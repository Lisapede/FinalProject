DINE & DRINK

Discover LA’s Most Unique Dining Experiences

The LINE LA is a culinary destination, offering unique dining spaces in Los Angeles for every taste. From sunrise to sunset, our restaurants go beyond dining with creative settings and vibrant entertainment. So, if you’re looking for fine dining in LA, enjoy flavorful dishes and a relaxing ambiance no matter where you dine in our boutique hotel.

Chef Spotlight

Artists in the Culinary World

Meet Chef Josiah Citrin and Chef Vinson Ching — the culinary minds behind the dining experiences at the LINE LA. Chef Josiah Citrin holds more than 25 years of culinary experience, including his accolade as a two-star Michelin Chef among other honors. Beyond the LINE LA, Chef Josiah Citrin has many renowned restaurants dotting LA’s corners, such as Citrin, Mélisse Restaurant, CHARCOAL Venice, and Augie’s on Main. He is also a chef on the team at Dear John’s and Dear Jane’s.

Adding to the excitement of the LINE LA’s dining experiences is Chef Ching, bringing cultural influences and techniques into his cooking. With experience as Executive Sous Chef at Wolfgang Puck, Chef de Cuisine at Workshop Kitchen & Bar, and Executive Chef at The Greyhound Bar & Grill, he holds years of expertise in the culinary world. Together this duo brings a fresh energy to every dish they craft at our Los Angeles restaurants.

OPENAIRE

Dine inspired at Openaire — a standout among restaurants in Los Angeles with a stunning greenhouse design. Led by Michelin-starred chef Josiah Citrin, every dish is crafted to perfection with an inventive touch. Whether you’re looking for the best breakfast in LA, a stylish brunch spot, or a romantic dinner setting, Openaire is the perfect dining choice after exploring LA’s top attractions like the Los Angeles County Museum of Art (LACMA) and The Grove.

LOBBY BAR

Need a space to unwind and raise a toast? Lobby Bar has you covered. Stylish and chic, our relaxing bar in Los Angeles offers light bites, craft cocktails, and decadent wines for your LA happy hour or social hour with friends. Every dish and beverage is curated by Chef Josiah Citrin and his team, offering a nod to the LINE LA’s signature dishes and cocktails. Laid-back, but never boring, you’ll find all your favorite classics on our menu.

ALFRED COFFEE

Say “Good morning, LA,” and kickstart your city adventure at the best coffee shop in Los Angeles. Treat your tastebuds to an aromatic cup of coffee or a refreshing matcha, paired with a breakfast bite to eat. Perfect for LA locals needing a place to work remotely or guests who like to fuel up before traveling around, Alfred is your go-to coffee stop. Indoor and outdoor seating is available.

BREAK ROOM 86

Step into a nostalgic corner of fun at Break Room 86, created by brothers Mark and Jonnie Houston. Inspired by the decor of their Koreatown childhood home, the Houston brothers bring the excitement of a music-filled space to this venue. Our vibrant hotspot offers handcrafted cocktails, live entertainment, and a retro-themed design so you can relax in 80s fashion. As one of the best bars in Los Angeles, nightlife in LA never felt so exciting. Plus, the best of karaoke in Koreatown awaits.

KISS KISS BANG BANG

Dance into a night of disco and creative cocktails at Kiss Kiss Bang Bang — LA’s favorite nightlife bar. Dress to the nines and enjoy a refreshing drink, take in the dazzling lights, and embrace the Gatsby-style feel. As a Houston brothers’ creation, this glamorous space carries the same speakeasy feel that’s reflected at Break Room 86 with an exciting twist. Blending the fun of the 70s with the style of the 20s, this lively hotspot is calling you by name.
